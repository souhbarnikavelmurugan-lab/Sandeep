To Sandeepkumar

Sandeepkumar, The past few days were not easy for us. In my overwhelm I pushed you away, and I even said things about ending what we have. But my heart never truly meant it. I am sorry for hurting you. What we share didn’t happen suddenly. It was built slowly — through years of friendship, patience, understanding, and all the little conversations only we know. You have seen every version of me — the happy one, the quiet one, the confused one — and you still stayed. That means more to me than I can properly express. Whenever everything inside me feels heavy and messy, I somehow find my way back to you. Talking to you steadies me and makes me feel like myself again. Your presence calms me in a way nothing else does. No matter how far my thoughts wander, I return to you… because with you I feel safe, understood, and at peace. I love you deeply, and I truly value us. You are summer, full of light, I was winter, cold as night. You were the warmth I longed to feel, While my heart stayed frozen, hard as steel. You stood by me, patient and kind, Melting the walls I tried to hide. Now I soften where once I froze — In your warmth, my heart glows.

Thank you for staying, for understanding me, and for being patient even when I struggled to understand myself. I don’t promise a perfect love… but I promise a real one, where I choose you every day.
